Our Story

At the heart of Burness Homes is a simple belief: every home should be thoughtfully crafted, built with precision, and delivered with integrity.

We started Burness Homes because we saw a gap in the market for builders who genuinely care about quality and communication. Too many builds end with surprises, compromises, and frustration. We wanted to change that.

Whether it's a bespoke architectural home or a streamlined family build, we bring the same level of care, attention, and professionalism to every project.
