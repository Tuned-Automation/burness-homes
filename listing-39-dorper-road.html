Listing Overview

A four-bedroom single-level home, designed around a north-facing deck.

Lot 642, Wilfield Rise — a new home on Dorper Road in West Melton. The architecture is shaped by a series of pitched gables clad in vertical b-rib colorsteel and vertical shiplap cedar weatherboards, with the double garage finished in a 60mm Integra plaster panel system.

Inside, the 225.97 m² plan is arranged across a single level — four bedrooms, a master with walk-in robe and ensuite, a family bathroom and powder room, plus separate living and media rooms flowing through to an open kitchen, dining, and rear deck on the northern aspect.
