Panama Crescent

Project Overview

A gable-form family home arranged around a sun-soaked central courtyard.

Set on a generous West Melton site, Panama Crescent is a single-level family home that pairs an architectural pitched gable with a wraparound veranda and a sheltered central courtyard. The form opens wide to the sun, with sliding doors on multiple aspects pulling the indoors out onto a hardwood deck.

Inside, five bedrooms sit alongside two living spaces, a designer walnut kitchen with a sculpted plaster rangehood, a freestanding wood burner, and a tiled family bathroom with a freestanding bath. The materials are warm and grounded — dark vertical shiplap timber, crisp white render, walnut joinery, and stone benchtops.
