01 / New Builds

New Builds

Bespoke homes designed and built from the ground up. From the first sketch through to handover, every aspect of your new build is managed personally by Brad and Chris.

We work with you and your architect to ensure the design is buildable, efficient, and exactly what you want. Then we build it with the care it deserves.

  • Full project management from start to finish
  • Architectural and bespoke design support
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden surprises
  • Regular updates and open communication
02 / Renovations & Alterations

Renovations &
Alterations

Thoughtful renovations that add value and improve how you live. Whether it's a kitchen transformation, a full home remodel, or an extension — no job is too big or too small.

We treat your occupied home with care and respect throughout the entire process.

  • Kitchen, bathroom, and living space upgrades
  • Extensions and additional rooms
  • Structural alterations and modernisation
  • Consenting and compliance managed for you
03 / Design & Build

Design & Build

A complete end-to-end service from concept to completion. We manage architectural design, consenting, construction, and project management — so you don't have to coordinate between multiple parties.

Ideal for clients who want a single point of contact and a seamless experience from day one.

  • Architectural design coordination
  • Building consent and council liaison
  • Construction and fit-out
  • Single point of contact throughout
