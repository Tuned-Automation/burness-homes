/ Services

What we do

From bespoke new builds to smart renovations and end-to-end design & build — we offer a complete range of residential construction services across Canterbury.

New Builds

Bespoke homes designed and built from the ground up. From the first sketch through to handover, every aspect of your new build is managed personally by Brad and Chris.

We work with you and your architect to ensure the design is buildable, efficient, and exactly what you want. Then we build it with the care and attention to detail it deserves.

  • Full project management from start to finish
  • Architectural and bespoke design support
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden surprises
  • Regular updates and open communication

Renovations & Alterations

Thoughtful renovations that add value and improve how you live. Whether it's a kitchen transformation, a full home remodel, or an extension — no job is too big or too small.

We understand that renovating an occupied home requires extra care and communication. We treat your home with the respect it deserves throughout the process.

  • Kitchen, bathroom, and living space upgrades
  • Extensions and additional rooms
  • Structural alterations and modernisation
  • Consenting and compliance managed for you

Design & Build

A complete end-to-end service that takes you from concept to completion. We manage architectural design, consenting, construction, and project management — so you don't have to coordinate between multiple parties.

This is our most comprehensive offering, ideal for clients who want a single point of contact and a seamless experience from day one.

  • Architectural design coordination
  • Building consent and council liaison
  • Construction and fit-out
  • Single point of contact throughout
